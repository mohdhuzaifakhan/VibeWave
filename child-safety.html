Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

Our Commitment

VibeWave takes the safety of children on our platform with the utmost seriousness. We are committed to keeping our community free of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We comply with the Google Play Child Safety Standards policy and work continuously to ensure a safe environment for all users.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

VibeWave has a zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation. We strictly prohibit:

  • The creation, sharing, or distribution of CSAM.
  • Any form of grooming or predatory behavior targeting minors.
  • Content that promotes or facilitates child exploitation.

Any account found to be engaging in such activities will be immediately and permanently banned, and we will report all relevant information to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and appropriate law enforcement authorities.

2. Reporting Mechanism

We provide multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:

In-App Reporting

Use the "Report" button on any user profile or message to flag content for child safety review. Reports are prioritized for immediate investigation.

Web Feedback

If you encounter child safety issues outside the app, please use our contact form or email our dedicated safety team directly.

3. Addressing CSAM

VibeWave utilizes a combination of automated technology and human moderation to identify and remove prohibited content. When CSAM is identified:

  • The content is removed immediately.
  • The account is terminated.
  • The material is preserved and reported to NCMEC's CyberTipline.

4. Legal Compliance

VibeWave complies with all applicable child safety laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction).

5. Child Safety Point of Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or need to report a child safety issue, please contact our dedicated Child Safety Officer:

Email:

safety@vibewave.app

Note: This email is strictly for child safety and exploitation reports. For general support, please use our help center.
