Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to VibeWave. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

VibeWave is a proximity-based social app. We collect high-precision GPS data to enable the "Radar" feature. This data is used to show you other users nearby and to show your profile to others based on your discovery settings.

Profile Information

We collect information you provide when creating a profile, including your name, interests, and profile pictures. This information is visible to other users within your discovery range.

Interest Data

We collect your selected interests to help filter and match you with like-minded individuals in your vicinity.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real-time social discovery via the Radar.
  • To facilitate "Waves" and mutual matching.
  • To operate the ephemeral chat system.
  • To enforce the Proximity Guard (range-based interactions).
  • To improve and optimize the user experience.

4. Ephemeral Messaging & Privacy

Chat rooms on VibeWave are ephemeral. Messages and chat rooms exist only as long as both users remain within discovery range (with a 1-hour grace period). Once the match expires, the chat history is permanently deleted from our active servers.

5. Your Controls

You have full control over your visibility on VibeWave:

  • Social Energy: Set your status to Open, Busy, or Private (Invisible).
  • Discovery Range: Adjust your search radius from 100m to 20km.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove all your data from our systems.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage users to be mindful of the information they share on their public profiles.

7. Child Safety

VibeWave is committed to the safety of children. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation. For more information, please review our full Child Safety Standards.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@vibewave.app.

For child safety concerns, please contact safety@vibewave.app.
